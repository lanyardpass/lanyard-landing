Privacy Policy

Effective April 9, 2026

The short version

We collect your email address when you join the waitlist. We use it for one purpose: to let you know when Lanyard launches on the App Store. That's all we do with it.

What we collect

When you submit the waitlist form on lanyardpass.com, we collect your email address. We do not collect your name, location, device information, or any other personal data.

How we use it

Your email address is used solely to send you a single notification when the Lanyard app is available on the App Store. We will not send you marketing emails, newsletters, or any other communications beyond that launch announcement.

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties. Our waitlist form is processed by Netlify, whose privacy policy governs how form submission data is handled on their platform.

How long we keep it

We retain your email address until Lanyard launches and the launch notification is sent, after which waitlist data is deleted. If you'd like your email removed before then, contact us at hello@lanyardpass.com and we'll remove it promptly.

The app itself

Lanyard is a local-first iOS app. All pass data, visit history, perk logs, and personal information you enter in the app are stored on your device. No account is required. Lanyard does not collect personal data, does not sell your information, and does not share it with third parties.

Anonymous usage analytics

The app uses TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous, aggregate usage signals — things like "the Perks tab was opened" or "a visit was logged." This helps us understand which features are actually useful so we can make the app better.

TelemetryDeck does not collect your name, email, location, or anything you've entered in the app. Signals are anonymized on your device before they're sent — we have no way to tie them back to you as an individual. No personal data ever leaves your phone.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@lanyardpass.com.
